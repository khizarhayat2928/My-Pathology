Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an account?

To register, click the "Register" button on the home page, fill out the form, and follow the email verification instructions.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email, and follow the instructions sent to your inbox.

How can I contact support?

Use the contact options below or email us directly at support@mypathology.com for immediate assistance.

What if I experience technical issues?

Try clearing your cache or reinstalling the app. If issues persist, contact our support team for further help.
